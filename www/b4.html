PRAYERS FOR THE FATIMA
CENTENARY AND MARIAN YEAR

QUEEN OF THE ROSARY, SWEET VIRGIN OF FATIMA,
WHO APPEARED IN THE LAND OF PORTUGAL AND
BROUGHT PEACE, BOTH INTERIOR AND EXTERIOR, TO
THAT ONCE SO TROUBLED COUNTRY.

WE BEG OF THEE, TO WATCH OVER OUR DEAR
HOMELAND AND TO ASSURE ITS MORAL, SPIRITUAL
AND ECONOMIC REVIVAL.

BRING BACK PEACE TO ALL NATIONS OF THE WORLD,
SO THAT ALL, AND OUR NATION IN PARTICULAR, MAY
BE HAPPY TO CALL THEE THEIR QUEEN AND THE
QUEEN OF PEACE.

OUR LADY OF THE ROSARY OF FATIMA, PRAY FOR OUR
COUNTRY.

OUR LADY OF FATIMA, OBTAIN FOR ALL HUMANITY A
DURABLE PEACE. AMEN.

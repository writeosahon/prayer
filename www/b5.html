THE MEMORARE

Remember, o most gracious Virgin Mary, that never was it known that anyone who fled to thy protection,
implore thy help or sought thy intercession was left unaided. Inspired by this confidence,
I fly unto thee, o virgin of virgins, my mother, to thee I come, before thee I stand sinful and sorrowful.
O mother of the word incarnate, despise not my petition but in thy mercy hear and answer me. Amen.

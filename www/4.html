3 Minutes, Nine Days Divine Mercy Novena

Fourth Day:

Today Bring to Me Those Who Do Not Believe in God and Those Who Do Not Yet Know Me.

Say one Our Father, One Hail Mary and Glory be to the Father

(Add your personal Intention)

 

Let's Pray
Most compassionate Jesus, You are the Light of the whole world. Receive into the abode of Your Most Compassionate
Heart the souls of those who do not believe in God and of those who as yet do not know You. Let the rays of Your grace enlighten them that they, too,
together with us, may extol Your wonderful mercy; and do not let them escape from the abode which is Your Most Compassionate Heart. 

 

Concluding Prayers
Eternal Father, turn Your merciful gaze upon the souls of those who do not believe in You, and of those who as yet do not know You,
but who are enclosed in the Most Compassionate Heart of Jesus. Draw them to the light of the Gospel.
These souls do not know what great happiness it is to love You. Grant that they, too, may extol the generosity of Your mercy for endless ages. Amen.
